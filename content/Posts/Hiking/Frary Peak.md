## Overview
Fourth hike in [Utah Range High Points](/Posts/Hiking/Utah%20Mountain%20Range%20High Points), also probably the most normal and uneventful of the bunch.

## The Hike
We started from the upper lot on antelope island. The hike was very simple compared to some of our recent hikes, which felt pretty good. As we could zone out and not worry about route finding. 
The hike was pretty steep in spots but in general wasn't crazy, really the perfect amount. 
It was a very busy hike, so Freddy had to remain on the leash. 
Near the top at a false summit, we mindlessly took the wrong turn and ended up traversing the rocky ridge about 300 yards to the summit. Which was much more advanced than the rest of the trail, but we realized this at the peak, and there is a perfectly easy trail that also gets there.

## Fred's Take
Fred, wasn't thrilled to be on the leash but that paled in comparison to being outside and seeing lots of people and dogs. Each of which he wanted us to stop for. The fun train stopped though when we went off the trail at the ridge. He had to be carried over a lot of rocks, and near the top we decided to just tie him to one and give him lunch while we ran to the peak. The food only distracted him for a bit though and he was angry. Luckily he forgot pretty quick once hitting the trail. In the end he gave the hike 7/10
